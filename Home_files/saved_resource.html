FAQ

Find answers to commonly asked questions

How does your hospital navigation system work?
Our hospital navigation system uses advanced indoor mapping and wayfinding technology to help patients and visitors navigate hospitals with ease. It provides real-time directions to help people find their way around hospital buildings and facilities.
What is the benefit of using Onesoft's hospital navigation system?
Our hospital navigation system is designed to improve patient experience, reduce anxiety, and enhance hospital operations. It helps patients and visitors find their way around hospitals quickly and easily, reducing stress and improving overall satisfaction.
How does your real-time navigation work?
Our real-time navigation uses advanced algorithms to provide up-to-the-minute information on hospital directions, wait times, appointments, and more. This helps patients and healthcare professionals make informed decisions and stay up-to-date on important information.
What is hospital wayfinding?
Hospital wayfinding is the process of helping patients and visitors navigate hospitals and healthcare facilities. Our hospital wayfinding solution uses advanced indoor mapping and wayfinding technology to provide patients with clear, easy-to-follow directions to their destination.
What types of hospitals can benefit from Onesoft's software solutions?
Our software solutions are designed to benefit hospitals of all sizes, including community hospitals, academic medical centers, and specialty hospitals. We work with healthcare organizations to create tailored solutions that meet their unique needs.
How can I get started using Onesoft's hospital navigation system?
We offer a range of implementation options and will work with you to create a customized solution that meets your hospital’s specific needs. Contact us to learn more about getting started with our hospital navigation system.
Do you offer training and support for your software solutions?
Yes, we provide comprehensive training and support for all of our software solutions. Our team is available to answer questions, provide technical support, and ensure that your hospital is getting the most out of our software.
How does Onesoft ensure data security and privacy?
We take data security and privacy very seriously and have stringent protocols in place to protect sensitive information. Our software solutions meet all HIPAA compliance standards and we use the latest encryption technology to ensure data remains secure.
